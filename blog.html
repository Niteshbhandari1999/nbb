Hotel Happiness

Along a tree-lined street, this casual hotel in a low-rise building with a mirrored-glass facade is a 2-minute walk from the Aircraft Museum and a 4-minute walk from Park. The H14 highway is 2 km away. Colourful rooms with wood floors offer free Wi-Fi. Upgraded rooms, some with furnished balconies or separate living areas, add minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Amenities include 2 casual restaurants with wood floors, a laid-back bar, and meeting and event space.
